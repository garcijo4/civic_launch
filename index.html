Why This Matters

Two shortages. One local system gap.

Students need paid experience.

A first-generation college student working two part-time jobs. Cannot afford an unpaid internship. Has the skills but not the access. Needs flexible, paid, career-relevant work that fits a real life.

Nonprofits need affordable capacity.

An executive director with a grant report, a website update, and a donor database cleanup on the to-do list. No budget for another hire. No bandwidth to manage an intern program. The work stays undone.

Civic Launch exists in the gap between these two unmet needs.
