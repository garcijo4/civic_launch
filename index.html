About Us

A local proof-of-concept designed to test what equitable early-career access can look like.

Civic Launch grew out of a simple mismatch in the ecosystem. The students who benefit most from paid experience often face the highest barriers to getting it. At the same time, nonprofit organizations often have valuable project work that never quite gets done because staffing and budget constraints are constant.

The pilot is deliberately narrow: Ventura County, nonprofit partners only, short-term projects, managed matching, and measured outcomes. That restraint is part of the design. Civic Launch is trying to prove a model carefully enough that it can be trusted, funded, and improved over time.

Mission-led

The work starts with nonprofit capacity and student equity, not with platform growth.

Human-guided

AI can improve preparation and productivity, but judgment, verification, and partner trust stay human.

Evidence-focused

The pilot is structured around measurable completion, satisfaction, return rates, and student outcomes.
