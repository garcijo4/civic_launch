# Civic Launch

Paid Micro-Internships. Nonprofit Capacity. Student Careers.

Civic Launch connects college students and recent graduates with short-term, paid, project-based work at nonprofit organizations. We scope the project, curate the match, handle W-2 payroll and compliance, and support quality from kickoff through final handoff.

**[Visit the Civic Launch Website](https://garcijo4.github.io/civic_launch/)**

---

### Project-based. Purpose-driven.

* **Nonprofits first:** The pilot serves nonprofit organizations only, keeping the first version focused and mission aligned.
* **Managed, not self-serve:** Project scoping, candidate review, onboarding, and quality support are handled through the intermediary.
* **W-2 compliant:** Students are paid workers, not volunteers and not independent contractors.
* **AI-enabled with guardrails:** Responsible AI support strengthens readiness and productivity, but hosts can opt out when appropriate.
